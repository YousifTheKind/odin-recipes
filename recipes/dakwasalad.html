Dakwa Salad

Description

Dakwa salad is a refreshing and vibrant dish made with a mix of peanut butter with fresh and colorful vegetables, such as lettuce, cucumber, tomatoes, bell peppers, and red onions.

The salad is typically tossed in a light and zesty vinaigrette dressing, which adds a tangy and flavorful element to the dish. Some variations of Dakwa salad may also include ingredients like olives, feta cheese, and herbs for added depth of flavor.

Ingredients

  • Mixed salad greens (such as baby spinach, arugula, and lettuce)
  • Cucumber, sliced
  • Peanut Butter
  • Cherry tomatoes, halved
  • Olive oil
  • Lemon Juice

Steps:

  1. Boil water in a large pot and add a pinch of salt.
  2. Wash and prepare the ingredients: Rinse and dry the mixed salad greens, cucumber, cherry tomatoes, red onion, and red bell pepper. Slice the cucumber, halve the cherry tomatoes, thinly slice the red onion, and dice the red bell pepper.
  3. Assemble the salad: In a large salad bowl, combine the mixed salad greens, sliced cucumber, halved cherry tomatoes, sliced red onion, and diced red bell pepper. Add the crumbled feta cheese and Kalamata olives on top.
  4. Make the dressing: In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper to taste. You can also add a pinch of dried oregano for extra flavor.
  5. Dress the salad: Drizzle the dressing over the salad and gently toss to coat the ingredients evenly.
