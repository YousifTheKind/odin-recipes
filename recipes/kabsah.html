Kabsah

Description

Kabsa or makbūs/machbūs is an Arab mixed rice dish that originates from Yemen.

It is commonly regarded as a national dish in all the countries of the Arabian Peninsula. It can also be found in regions such as southern Iran and Gaza in Palestine

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Chicken or meat
  • Vegetables

Steps:

  1. Prepare the meat and spices
  2. Add the vegetables
  3. Add the rice and liquid
  4. Cook the kabsa
  5. Serve the kabsa
